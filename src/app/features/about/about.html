About Fitness AI

Your smart AI powered fitness companion for workouts, diet planning and progress tracking.

Who We Are

Fitness AI is a modern web application designed to help users improve their health and fitness using technology. Our platform combines artificial intelligence with fitness science to generate personalized workout plans, diet suggestions and progress tracking.

Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, or simply stay fit, Fitness AI provides tools that help you stay consistent and motivated.

What Fitness AI Offers

AI Trainer

Get AI generated workout plans based on your fitness goals.

Workout Library

Explore exercises with videos, sets, reps and instructions.

Diet Planner

Generate diet plans for weight loss, muscle gain and fitness.

Progress Tracking

Track your weight, BMI and workout history easily.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make fitness accessible for everyone. With the help of AI and smart tracking tools, we aim to help users stay consistent and achieve their health goals faster.
